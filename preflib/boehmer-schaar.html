Data Collected by Niclas Boehmer and Nathan Schaar

This page provides more details about the data collected by Niclas Boehmer and Nathan Schaar in their paper Collecting, Classifying, Analyzing, and Using Real-World Elections.

We recommend you to have a careful look at the paper to understand how the strict preferences were obtained.

The Datasets

Here is a list of the datasets:

  • Boardgames Geek Ranking
  • Boxing
  • Cycling Races
  • Table Tennis Ranking
  • Tennis Ranking
  • Global University Ranking
  • Spotify Daily Chart
  • Spotify Countries Chart
  • Multilaps Competitions
  • Movehub City Ranking
  • Countries Ranking
  • Formula 1 Seasons
  • Formula 1 Races
  • Weeks Power Ranking
  • Combined Power Ranking
  • Seasons Power Ranking
